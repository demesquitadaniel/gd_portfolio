12-Day Brazil Circuit

The Brief

A US-based events agency needed a fully custom luxury itinerary proposal for 18 C-suite executives across four Brazilian destinations — Rio de Janeiro, Iguaçu Falls, the Pantanal, and São Paulo. Deadline: 48 hours. Budget tier: ultra-premium.

My Approach

Built a 32-slide branded deck from scratch: custom cover with client's logo, Compass Brazil overview, full itinerary map, day-by-day breakdowns (morning · lunch · afternoon · dinner · evening), curated photography per destination, and a tiered pricing table. Delivered within 36 hours.
